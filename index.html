The Problem

Most supplements hide what's really inside

The supplement industry is full of products packed with fillers, binders, and unknown substances in capsules — many of which are allergenic, unlisted, and potentially harmful.

💊
Hidden fillers in capsules
Most commercial capsules contain fillers like magnesium stearate, silicon dioxide, titanium dioxide, and artificial coatings — none of which appear on the front label.
⚠️
Allergenic ingredients
Fillers and binders commonly contain gluten, soy, dairy derivatives, shellfish, and tree nut-derived compounds — serious risks for allergy sufferers who have no way of knowing.
❓
Unknown substances
Proprietary blends legally allow manufacturers to hide individual ingredient amounts. Third-party testing regularly finds unlisted substances, contaminants, and dose discrepancies in popular brands.
✅

What We Stand For

Our core principles

🔬
Evidence first, always
Every ingredient is selected based on peer-reviewed clinical trials and systematic reviews — not anecdote, tradition, or marketing potential. We rank ingredients based on strength of clinical evidence — from multiple RCTs and meta-analyses to strong mechanistic support.
⚖️
Therapeutic doses, not label doses
There is a legal minimum needed to print a claim on a label, and there is the dose actually used in clinical trials. These are often very different numbers. We use the latter — every time.
🎯
Personalized, not generic
Your health concerns, your symptom severity, your medication list, your life stage. Your formula is built around you specifically — not a one-size-fits-all product designed to appeal to everyone.
🔒
Pharmacist reviewed, every time
Before your formula is prepared, a licensed pharmacist reviews your ingredient selection and screens for interactions with your medications. This is not optional — it is built into every single order.
🍁
Health Canada compliant
Every ingredient in our library is a confirmed Health Canada Schedule 1 Natural Health Product. We work exclusively within the regulatory framework designed to protect Canadian consumers.
📖
Radical transparency
No proprietary blends. No hidden doses. Every ingredient in your formula comes with its dose, its mechanism of action, and the clinical evidence behind why it was recommended for you.
🚫
Zero fillers. Zero unknowns.
Most commercial capsules are packed with fillers, binders, and allergenic substances that never appear on the front label. NutriLab formulas are compounded fresh with no fillers, no binders, no artificial coatings — and a certificate of analysis for every single ingredient.
🌿
Allergy-safe by design
Common supplement fillers contain hidden gluten, soy, dairy, and shellfish derivatives — serious risks for allergy sufferers. Because we compound fresh to your formula, there are no hidden excipients and no cross-contamination from irrelevant ingredients.

What is Natural Health Product compounding?
Compounding is the practice of creating a personalized Natural Health Product (NHP) by a licensed pharmacist — tailored to the specific needs of an individual patient rather than mass-produced for the general population.
🏥
Health Canada regulated
NutriLab formulas are compounded in accordance with Health Canada's NHP Compounding Policy at CompoundIt Pharmacy — a licensed NHP compounding facility in Aurora, Ontario.
📋
Why it matters for supplements
Compounding allows your dose to be calibrated precisely to your symptom severity, your allergen profile, and your medication interactions — something no mass-produced product can offer.
